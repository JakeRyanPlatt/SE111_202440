This movie follows Caden Cotard (Philip Seymour Hoffman) a theater director, husband and father to Adele (Catherine Keener) and daughters name (Sadie Goldstein). Caden is obsessed with his health and it severages his marriage and relationship with his daughter. His therapist is only interesting in selling her book. In a desperate attempt to make a great theater piece they flee to New York City from Schenectady, New York. Here he creates a one to one replica of New York City and has actors play their own lives within the constructed city in addtion to casting himself and people in Cadens life.

Release Date: October 2008

Director/Writer: Charlie Kaufman

Runtime: 2hr 4m

Genre: Drama

Rating: 7.5/10
